About IGNITE 2.0

IGNITE 2.0 is an interclass technical and cultural fest organized by the SY BCA students of SPES Goa Multi-Faculty College, Dharbandora Goa. The event brings together students from different classes to compete, collaborate, and showcase their talents across coding, creativity, gaming, and performance-based events. IGNITE aims to promote innovation, teamwork, leadership, and healthy competition while creating a vibrant platform for students to learn, connect, and celebrate skills beyond the classroom.
